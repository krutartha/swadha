About Us

Swadhā (स्वधा) — a sacred offering, a return to essence.

Swadhā Life is a conscious wellness brand creating biodegradable, design-led guest essentials for the world's most discerning hospitality spaces.

Rooted in India's cultural and artisanal heritage, our products elevate the guest experience while reflecting your brand's sustainability and storytelling.

Each kit is more than an amenity — it's a tactile expression of care, tradition, and intention. Crafted with skilled artisans and women-led self-help groups, our offerings unite minimalist elegance, ethical craftsmanship, and cultural authenticity.

We help luxury hotels and retreats replace plastic with purpose, creating meaningful guest touchpoints that honour both the environment and the spirit of place.

Mission · Vision · Purpose

MISSION

To enrich every guest experience with consciously crafted wellness kits that embody India's timeless wisdom, celebrate artisan skill, and reflect care for the planet.

VISION

To set a global standard in wellness and lifestyle where Indian wisdom guides health, personal care, empowerment, and care for the planet.

PURPOSE

To revive traditional Indian wellness through eco-luxury products that nurture body, mind, and soul. To empower women artisans, honor the planet, and inspire a global shift toward conscious, holistic living rooted in timeless wisdom.
